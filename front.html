Space technology

**Space Technology** refers to the tools, systems, and scientific methods developed to explore, understand, and utilize outer space. It includes satellites, spacecraft, rockets, space stations, and ground systems used for communication, navigation, weather forecasting, and scientific research. Space technology plays a vital role in modern life by enabling GPS, satellite TV, internet services, and environmental monitoring. It also supports planetary exploration and advances in astronomy. Developed through engineering and innovation, space technology contributes to global development, defense, and disaster management, while inspiring new discoveries about our universe and the potential for future human settlement beyond Earth.
